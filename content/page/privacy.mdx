**Effective Date:** January 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Web Academy ("we" or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information from users of our website (the "Site").

## Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users of the Site:

- **Log Data:** We automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity when you visit our website. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages you visit, time of access, and referring website.
- **Post Views:** We track the number of times each post is viewed by each user's IP address. This data is stored in our database for analytics purposes.
- **Newsletter Subscriptions:** If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, we will collect your email address.

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

- **Log Data:** We use log data to analyze website traffic and improve our website's performance and content.
- **Post Views:** We use post view data to understand which topics are most popular with our readers and to tailor our content accordingly.
- **Newsletter Subscriptions:** We use your email address to send you our newsletter with updates about new posts and other relevant information.

## How We Share Information

We will not share your personal information with any third-party companies or individuals, except as follows:

- **Service Providers:** We may share your information with service providers who help us operate our website, such as web hosting providers and analytics providers.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.

## Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

- **Cookies:** You can control how cookies are used by adjusting your browser settings.
- **Newsletter:** You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of each newsletter email.
- **Data Access and Deletion:** You can request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at [ikyawthetpaing@gmail.com](mailto:ikyawthetpaing@gmail.com).

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track your activity on our website and to hold certain information. Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to remember your preferences and settings, and to understand how you interact with our website. You can control how cookies are used by adjusting your browser settings.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

## Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, as set forth in this Privacy Policy. We will then delete your personal information unless we are required to keep it for legal reasons.

## Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [email protected] We will delete your child's information from our records.

## International Users

If you are a user of the Site located outside of the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. The United States may have data protection laws that differ from the laws of your country. By using the Site, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on the Site so that you can always know what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

## Contact Us
