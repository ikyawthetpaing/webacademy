We're passionate about crafting insightful and engaging articles for you. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies ("Cookies") to serve you the best possible experience on our website ("Site").

## What are Cookies?

Imagine Cookies as tiny, helpful crumbs your browser stores when you visit a website. They remember your preferences (like font size) and track your activities (like reading our latest article on astrophysics).

## What Cookies Do We Use?

Web Academy uses different Cookies for various purposes:

- **Essential Cookies:** These Cookies are crucial for the Site to function properly. They enable you to navigate seamlessly and remember your basic preferences like language and region. Without these Cookies, some features, like our "Remember Me" option for newsletter subscriptions, wouldn't work.

- **Analytics Cookies:** These Cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the Site. They provide anonymous data on things like page views, bounce rates, and time spent on each page. This information is invaluable for improving the Site's content and layout to better serve your needs.

- **Functionality Cookies:** These Cookies personalize your experience on Web Academy. They remember your choices, like whether you preferred dark or light mode on your last visit, and keep you logged in if you subscribe to our newsletter.

- **Advertising Cookies:** We currently do not use third-party advertising Cookies on Web Academy. However, we may occasionally partner with trusted advertisers in the future, and some of their Cookies may be placed on your device through our Site. We'll be sure to update this Policy and clearly explain any future use of advertising Cookies.

## Your Cookie Choices:

You have complete control over the Cookies we use on your device. You can manage your preferences through your browser settings, choosing to accept, reject, or delete Cookies. Please note that disabling certain Cookies may impact your experience on the Site.

Here are some helpful resources for managing Cookies in popular browsers:

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/block-websites-storing-cookies-site-data-firefox)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/105082)
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
